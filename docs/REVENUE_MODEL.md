## 1. Executive Summary

ConTigo's revenue model is a **B2B SaaS subscription** with three tiers, implementation services, and expansion add-ons. The model is designed around a **blended ACV of ~CHF 30,000/year** per customer — reflecting the Swiss enterprise market's willingness to pay for premium, compliance-native CLM software.

### Key Financial Highlights

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 | Year 4 | Year 5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Customers (EoY)** | 20 | 80 | 200 | 400 | 700 |
| **Blended ACV** | CHF 25,000 | CHF 29,000 | CHF 33,000 | CHF 36,000 | CHF 39,000 |
| **ARR** | CHF 500,000 | CHF 2,320,000 | CHF 6,600,000 | CHF 14,400,000 | CHF 27,300,000 |
| **Total Revenue** | CHF 580,000 | CHF 2,620,000 | CHF 7,200,000 | CHF 15,400,000 | CHF 29,100,000 |
| **Gross Margin** | 84% | 87% | 89% | 90% | 91% |
| **EBITDA** | (CHF 220,000) | CHF 120,000 | CHF 1,800,000 | CHF 5,300,000 | CHF 11,600,000 |
| **EBITDA Margin** | -38% | 5% | 25% | 34% | 40% |

> **Core thesis:** At ~CHF 30K ACV with 90%+ gross margins and <10% annual logo churn, ConTigo achieves profitability in Year 2 and >40% EBITDA margins at scale — positioning it as a capital-efficient, Swiss-domiciled SaaS business.

## 10. Customer Lifetime Value Model

### 10.1 LTV Calculation

LTV is calculated using the standard DCF-based formula adjusted for expansion revenue:

$$LTV = \frac{ACV \times Gross\ Margin \times NRR}{Annual\ Churn\ Rate + Discount\ Rate}$$

### 10.2 LTV by Segment

| Segment | ACV | GM | NRR | Churn | Discount Rate | **LTV** | **LTV:CAC** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | CHF 5,400 | 94% | 102% | 15% | 10% | **CHF 20,700** | 5.9x |
| Professional | CHF 16,400 | 95% | 112% | 8% | 10% | **CHF 86,800** | 12.4x |
| Enterprise | CHF 54,000 | 96% | 125% | 4% | 10% | **CHF 370,300** | 24.7x |
| **Blended** | **CHF 25,000** | **95%** | **110%** | **8%** | **10%** | **CHF 131,900** | **18.8x** |

| Metric | Cohort Y1 | Cohort Y2 | Cohort Y3 |
|---|---|---|---|

## 11. Break-Even Analysis

### 11.1 Monthly Break-Even Point

| Fixed Costs (Monthly) | CHF |
|---|---|
| Engineering (3 FTEs avg, Year 1) | 25,000 |
| Founders (2, reduced salary) | 16,000 |
| Sales & marketing | 10,000 |
| G&A (legal, accounting, office) | 8,000 |
| Infrastructure (base platform) | 3,500 |
| **Total Monthly Fixed Costs** | **CHF 62,500** |

$$Break\text{-}even\ MRR = \frac{Monthly\ Fixed\ Costs}{Gross\ Margin\ \%} = \frac{62,500}{0.84} = CHF\ 74,400$$

**Break-even MRR: ~CHF 74,400/month**

This requires approximately:

| Plan Mix | Customers Needed |
|---|---|
| All Starter | 152 customers |
