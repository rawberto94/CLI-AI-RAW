## 1. Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles

| Principle | Rationale |
|---|---|
| **CHF-native pricing** | No FX surprises for Swiss/DACH clients; signals local commitment |
| **Value-based, not seat-based** | Price on contracts managed (value delivered), not the number of users logging in |
| **Generous user limits** | Removes adoption friction — more users = more stickiness |
| **Transparent & predictable** | No hidden overage bombs; clear limits with pre-agreed upgrade paths |
| **Annual incentive** | 2 months free on annual plans (16.7% effective discount) drives cash upfront |
| **Enterprise flexibility** | Custom pricing for >100 users / >10K contracts — allows deal negotiation |

### Swiss Market Pricing Context

Swiss enterprise software buyers expect:

- **Premium pricing** for quality — Switzerland is **not** a discount market
- **Local invoicing in CHF** with Swiss VAT (MWST) compliance
- **Swiss entity** on invoices (ConTigo GmbH, CHE-xxx.xxx.xxx)
- **No surprise USD/EUR pricing conversions** on renewal
- **Personal relationship** with account management (Enterprise segment)
- **Swiss FADP compliance included by default**, not as a paid add-on

## 8. Customer ROI Calculator

### 8.1 ROI Model (Sales Tool)

Use this model during sales conversations to demonstrate value. Input customer-specific numbers:

#### Input Variables

| Variable | Description | Default |
|---|---|---|
| `N` | Number of active contracts | 1,000 |
| `H` | Hours spent per contract review (manual) | 3 hours |
| `R` | Fully-loaded hourly rate of reviewer (CHF) | CHF 120 |
| `M` | Missed renewal rate (% of portfolio value) | 5% |
| `V` | Average annual contract value (CHF) | CHF 50,000 |
| `P` | ConTigo annual subscription cost (CHF) | CHF 14,900 |

#### Output Calculations

| Metric | Formula | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Manual review cost** | `N × H × R` | 1,000 × 3 × 120 = **CHF 360,000** |
| **AI-assisted review cost** | `N × 0.3H × R` (70% faster) | 1,000 × 0.9 × 120 = **CHF 108,000** |
| **Review savings** | Manual – AI cost | **CHF 252,000** |
| **Missed renewal recovery** | `N × M × V` | 1,000 × 5% × 50,000 = **CHF 2,500,000** |
| **Realistic recovery (20%)** | 20% of preventable missed renewals | **CHF 500,000** |
| **Total annual benefit** | Review savings + Recovery | **CHF 752,000** |
| **ConTigo annual cost** | Subscription + implementation | **CHF 17,900** (incl. CHF 3K implementation) |
| **Net ROI** | (Benefit – Cost) / Cost | **4,103%** |
| **Payback period** | Cost / (Benefit ÷ 12) | **< 1 month** |

- Annual upgrade during trial: Additional 5% off Year 1

### 9.2 Downgrade Policy

| From | To | Conditions | Data Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Professional** | Starter | 30 days notice; effective at renewal | Excess contracts archived (read-only) |
| **Enterprise** | Professional | 60 days notice; effective at renewal | Custom features disabled; data retained |
| **Annual** | Monthly | Effective at annual renewal only | No change |

**Downgrade safeguards:**

- If contract count exceeds new plan limit: excess contracts become **read-only** (not deleted)
- Customer data is **never deleted** on downgrade — always accessible in read-only
- 90-day re-upgrade window at original pricing

| Event | Trigger | Action |

| **Account cancellation** | Customer cancels | Access until end of billing period; data export available |
| **Data deletion** | 90 days after cancellation | All customer data permanently deleted (FADP-compliant) |
| **Account reactivation** | Former customer returns | Data restored if within 90-day window; new contract required |

### 17.1 Swiss OR Accounting Treatment

|---|---|---|
| **Monthly subscription** | Recognised in the month of service delivery | OR Art. 958 ff. |
| **Annual subscription (prepaid)** | Deferred; recognised monthly (1/12 per month) | Abgrenzungsposten (accrued revenue) |
| **Implementation fees** | Recognised upon completion of onboarding | OR Art. 958 |
| **Training services** | Recognised upon delivery | OR Art. 958 |
| **Custom integrations** | Percentage of completion or upon delivery | OR Art. 958 |

### 17.2 SaaS Metrics Calculation Standards

| Metric | Calculation | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **ARR** | Sum of all active annual subscription values | Includes annual equivalent of monthly contracts |
| **MRR** | ARR ÷ 12 | Used for internal tracking |
| **NRR (Net Revenue Retention)** | (Starting MRR + expansion – contraction – churn) / Starting MRR | Trailing 12 months |
| **Gross churn** | Lost MRR / Starting MRR | Monthly calculation, annualised |
| **ACV** | Total contract value ÷ contract years | Normalised to 12 months |
| **Deferred revenue** | Prepaid subscription not yet recognised | Balance sheet liability |
